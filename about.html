About Us

We are a great community that supports people from all walks of life. With over 300+ students, we aim to welcome even more students to our university because we aim to normalize education.

The Totally Real & Not Fake Univeristy was founded in 1971 by a group of professors with a great vision. We are located on the shores of Vancouver Island, overlooking the ocean and with a great trail around campus. We have student apartments available on campus; can be shared or single.
